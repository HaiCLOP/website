About Discord Azure Bot

The Discord Azure Bot is a powerful and versatile bot designed to enhance your Discord server experience. Developed with the latest technologies, our bot offers a wide range of features to streamline moderation, entertainment, and community engagement.

Our mission is to provide Discord server owners and administrators with a reliable and customizable bot solution that meets their specific needs. Whether you're managing a small community or a large gaming server, our bot has you covered.

At Discord Azure, we are committed to continuous improvement and innovation. We value feedback from our users and actively incorporate it into our development process to ensure we deliver the best possible experience.

Bot Commands

  1. !ping: Responds with "Pong!" when invoked.
  2. !botinvite: Generates an invite link for the bot.
  3. !invite: Sends a never-expiring server invite link.
  4. !kick: Kicks a member from the server.
  5. !memberstatus: Displays the count of members based on their statuses.
  6. !rgive: Gives a role to a member.
  7. !rrm: Removes a role from a member.
  8. !ban: Bans a member from the server.
  9. !unban: Unbans a user from the server.
  10. !warn: Warns a member for breaking a rule.
  11. !clear: Deletes a specified number of messages from the channel.
  12. !nick: Changes the nickname of a member.
  13. !mute: Mutes a member for a specified amount of time.
  14. !serverinfo: Displays information about the server.
  15. !echo: Repeats the message sent by the user.
  16. !roll: Generates a random number between two integers.
  17. !afk: Sets the AFK status with an optional message.
  18. !unafk: Clears the AFK status and sets the user's nickname to their default username.
  19. !userinfo: Displays information about the user who invoked the command.
  20. !ticket: Opens a ticket.
  21. !addkeyword: Adds a keyword to the list of keywords to watch for.
  22. !reset: Resets warnings for a specific user.
